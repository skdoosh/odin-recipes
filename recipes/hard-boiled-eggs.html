Perfect Hard Boiled Eggs

Description

Make hard-boiled eggs with my method that makes the most perfect eggs ever â€” and they're easy to peel. The whites are firm but not rubbery, and the yolks are cooked and still creamy.

Ingredients

  • 6 medium sized eggs

Instructions

  1. Place eggs in a saucepan and pour in cold water to cover; place over high heat.
  2. When water starts to simmer and eggs start to dance around a little, turn off heat, cover the pan quickly with a lid, and let stand for 17 minutes. Don't peek.
  3. Pour out hot water and pour cold water over the eggs. Drain and refill with cold water; let stand until eggs are cool, about 20 minutes.
  4. Peel eggs under running water.
