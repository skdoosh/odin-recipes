Fluffy Microwave Scrambled Eggs

Description

Fluffy microwave scrambled eggs offer a quick and convenient way to enjoy a delicious breakfast or snack without needing to use a stovetop. To make them, simply whisk together eggs, a splash of milk, and a pinch of salt and pepper in a microwave-safe bowl. The mixture is then cooked in short intervals, usually around 30 seconds at a time, stirring between each interval to ensure even cooking and to achieve a light, airy texture. The result is a soft and fluffy scramble that's ready in just a few minutes.

This method of cooking scrambled eggs is not only time-efficient but also minimizes cleanup, as the eggs are prepared in the same bowl used for mixing. For added flavor and variety, you can mix in cheese, herbs, or cooked vegetables before microwaving. The microwave’s gentle heat helps to retain the eggs' moisture, preventing them from becoming dry or rubbery, which can sometimes happen with traditional cooking methods. Whether you're in a hurry or just prefer an easy meal, fluffy microwave scrambled eggs provide a tasty and satisfying option.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • ¼ cup milk
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Break the eggs into a microwave-proof mixing bowl. Add milk and salt; mix well.
  3. Pop the bowl into the microwave and cook on high power for 30 seconds.
  4. Remove the bowl, beat the eggs very well, scraping down the sides of the bowl, and return to the microwave for another 30 seconds.
  5. Repeat this pattern, stirring every 30 seconds for up to 2 1/2 minutes. Stop when eggs have the consistency you desire.
  6. Serve warm and enjoy!
